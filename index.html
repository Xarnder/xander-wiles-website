About Me

"Curious. Ambitious. Technology Lover."

Hi, I'm Xander. I'm a 23-year-old software engineer and artist from the UK. I hold a degree in Computer Animation and Masters in Artificial Intelligence.

I love bringing ideas to life, whether that’s coding up a new web app, designing a game, or training an AI Model. When I’m not at the keyboard, you’ll find me with my Lumix S5 in hand or adding to my journal, which recently hit over 1,000 entries!
