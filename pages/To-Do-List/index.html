Time Automations

Time Automations allow lists to automatically forward tasks to another list.

After Time in List:

Moves a task as soon as it reaches the specified age within the list. (e.g. Move after 2 hours)

On a specific Schedule:

Checks the list at the Time of Day you set, moving any tasks that meet your age requirements.

Pro Tip: Set Schedule to 'Daily' at '00:00', and Age to '0', to migrate all tasks in a 'Tomorrow' list to a 'Today' list every Midnight!
