Detailed Instructions

01

Initial Configuration

Open the extension popup and set your **Master Password**. This is the key to revealing any hidden page. Without it, the extension controls remain disabled for security.

02

Manage your Blacklist

Use the "Automatic List" box to type in domains like youtube.com or reddit.com. Click Save. These sites will now trigger the decoy screen the moment they load.

03

Using Temporary Access

When you need to browse freely without constant password prompts, enter a duration in the **Temporary Access** section and click Start. A green countdown will appear, and all hiding logic is paused until the time runs out.

04

The Recovery Process

To reveal a page, click the **Reload** button on the decoy. A browser prompt will appear. Enter your password. If correct, the decoy vanishes and your content returns.

04

Use the Timer

For quick browsing sessions, enter a duration (e.g., 5 minutes) in the popup. This unhides everything instantly without needing to enter your password for every tab.

Security & Privacy Disclaimer

Auto Hide Page is a visual privacy tool. It is designed to hide your activity from people physically near your computer. Important: This extension does not hide your history from your Internet Service Provider (ISP) or hide your IP address. It is not a VPN or an encryption tool. Your Master Password is stored locally on your device.
