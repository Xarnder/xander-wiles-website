Her Story

Siewli was warmth, strength, and humour. She loved lilies, quiet kindness, and making people feel seen. Though cancer took her in 2015, the way she loved remains.

What She Loved

Family dinners, deep chats, small surprises, and that rich burgundy tone that felt both calm and bold.

How We Remember

In everyday moments and shared meals, generous words, gentle courage. May these photos and stories keep her light close to us.
